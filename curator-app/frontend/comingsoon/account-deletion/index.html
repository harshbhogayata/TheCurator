Account deletion

Delete your Curator account and data.

You can delete your account inside The Curator app. If you cannot access the app, email support from the account email address and we will process the request.

Delete in the app

  1. Open The Curator and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings or Account.
  3. Choose Delete account and confirm the prompt.

Request deletion by email

Send your account email and the phrase "Please delete my Curator account" to support.

Email deletion request

What is deleted

We delete or anonymize your account profile, saved articles, collections, preferences, reading history, and linked identity data. Some records may be retained only when required for security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, legal, or compliance obligations.
