Privacy policy

Your data stays practical, limited, and reviewable.

The Curator collects only what is needed to create an account, sync your reading library, provide subscriptions, improve reliability, and respond to support requests.

Information we process

  • Account details such as email, display name, Firebase user ID, and authentication provider.
  • App activity such as saved articles, collections, reading progress, preferences, and search interactions.
  • Purchase and entitlement data from app-store billing and RevenueCat.
  • Device and diagnostic data used for crash reporting, notifications, fraud prevention, and support.

How we use it

We use this data to operate The Curator, personalize your experience, sync your account, deliver notifications you enable, manage subscriptions, fix bugs, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers that help operate the app, such as Firebase, hosting providers, RevenueCat, Sentry, email/support tools, and app stores when needed for purchases or compliance.

Control

You can request export or deletion from inside the app or through the account deletion page. Deleting your account removes or anonymizes personal data unless retention is required for legal, security, or billing reasons.

Contact

Email privacy@thecurator.com for privacy requests.
